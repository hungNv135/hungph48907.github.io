    Beef pho


    Beef pho, the quintessence of Vietnamese cuisine, is a spiritual gift for diners. The rich, sweet broth from stewed bones blends with soft, chewy pho noodles and fragrant beef, creating an unforgettable flavor. Herbs, chili lime, and elegant bean sprouts add to the dish. Beef pho is not only delicious but also contains cultural value, a symbol of togetherness and cohesion

    Banh trang


    Banh trang, a rustic dish associated with the childhood of many Vietnamese people. The thin, chewy crust is made from rice flour and coated in a hot pan, giving it a rustic, simple flavor. Banh trang can be eaten with many different dishes, from boiled meat, raw vegetables to rolls such as spring rolls and spring rolls.

    Banh mi


    Banh mi, Vietnam's famous street food, is a symphony of flavors. Crispy crust, filled with fragrant grilled meat, fatty pate, cool herbs with cucumber, pickles, and hot chili. Each sandwich is a colorful picture, providing a unique culinary experience.

    Enjoy the cuisine


    It's not just about eating and drinking, but also a journey to discover the culture, history and people of the place we visit. Each dish carries its own story, containing the unique characteristics of the region, expressed through cooking methods, ingredients and typical flavors.

    Banh xeo


    Banh xeo, a famous Vietnamese delicacy, is a golden, crispy symphony that evokes all senses. The thin crust of the cake, embracing the filling of minced meat, fresh shrimp, bean sprouts and herbs, blends with the fatty taste of coconut milk, creating an irresistible appeal.

    Spring rolls


    Spring rolls, a refreshing, colorful dish, are a gift from Vietnam. The thin rice paper holds the filling of pork, fresh shrimp, raw vegetables, and vermicelli, creating a harmonious, elegant flavor. The sweet and sour and spicy dipping sauce adds to the appeal of the dish.

    Sushi


    Sushi, a world-famous Japanese dish, is a delicate symphony of flavors. Fragrant rice mixed with vinegar envelops the fresh seafood filling, mixed with the spicy taste of wasabi and the sweetness of ginger. Each sushi roll is a work of art, providing a unique culinary experience.
